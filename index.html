What is Parent's Choice? ∨
Parent's Choice is Walmart's trusted baby brand -- offering diapers, training pants, wipes, formula, foods, feeding essentials, bedding and bath products designed to give every family quality care at prices they can count on.
What does "It takes a village" mean for Parent's Choice? ∨
Beyond making great products, Parent's Choice is built on a belief that no parent should feel alone. The brand supports a community of caregivers, offers real parent reviews, and continuously improves based on family feedback.
Are Parent's Choice diapers fragrance-free? ∨
Yes -- the Sensitive line is fragrance-free and hypoallergenic, dermatologist-tested for babies with sensitive skin. Standard diapers are also designed to be gentle, with a soft inner layer and leakproof protection.
What sizes do Parent's Choice diapers and training pants come in? ∨
Diapers are available from Newborn through Size 6. Training pants are available in 2T–3T, 3T–4T, and 4T–5T in both girls' and boys' styles, plus overnight training pants.
